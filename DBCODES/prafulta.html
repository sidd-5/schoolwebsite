Enables students to make informed decisions about their career options. Career Guidance (CG) testing is conducted for students of 9th and 11th standards. An expert Career Counselor conducts the tests, generates reports, and assists students and parents in choosing the right option.

It's mostly 'One on One'(one special educator with one student) and group therapy sessions to help with' Social and Communication skills'.
Generally, 2-3 sessions per week are suggested.

Structured remedial programs help manage and enhance skills like reading, writing, and math, transferring these skills to school performance, improving social skills, and addressing behavioral problems with focused attention and control.

The students who approach us with the above mentioned difficulties are helped with structured 'Remedial Program' which enables
(a)Management and Enhancement of skills like Reading, Writing and Math.
(b) Transference of skills in to school performance.
(c) Improved Social Skills.
(d) Focused attention and control in behavioral problem

Procedure starts with an 'Informal assessment' by the special educator.
A formal assessment (Psycho educational assessment) will be conducted by an 'Educational Psychologist' to have the proper diagnosis of the exact difficulties being faced by the child.
Based on the above results, an IEP (Individualized Education Plan) is generated for a specific period of time (3 - 6 months).
An IEP is shared with the parents and teachers, to make the child's learning process more effective.
During this process, we take the help of 'Multi modal therapy', involving other therapists like Occupational therapists & Speech therapists.
At the end of the specified time period, a progress report is generated.
