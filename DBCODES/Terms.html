Terms and Conditions for Online Payment Gateway

Detailed instructions are given on the login page of the web payment as to the procedure to be followed in case of a completed transaction and payment confirmation slip received, and also for failed transactions. Please follow the instructions carefully.

Once the “PAY” option is selected, you will be directed for payment through net banking, debit/credit card, online banking, or UPI. You can choose the desired payment option and proceed.


Once the payment is successful, you will receive a payment confirmation slip, which the candidate needs to keep for reference. In case the payment is not successful due to any reason, you will see a display of the status of failure in payment.

If the payment is debited/successful but you do not get the proper response in the HiGrade portal, please wait for 12 to 24 hours for the update. If successful payment does not show any acknowledgment, please wait for 15 to 30 minutes and then try again if the payment failed; otherwise, contact the support team.


If none of the above scenarios happens and there is a significant delay in response, do not proceed with payment if you have not entered your bank/payment details. Restart the payment process from the beginning. If you have already authorized payment and have not received a response, please check with your bank or credit card company to confirm if your account has been debited. If debited, do not attempt payment again.

However, if your account is not debited, you must retry the payment process and obtain a successful payment confirmation. Always make a note of reference/transaction details in case of net banking, card payments, or UPI.
